Independence of a collection of events

e Intuitive “definition”: Information on some of the events
does not change probabilities related to the remaining events

Definition: Events Aj, Ao9,...,An are called independent if:

P(A; N.AjN:++:N Am) = P(A;)P(A;):::P(Am) for any distinct indices i,j,...,m

n= 3:
P(A1M Az) = P(A1) - P(A2)

P(A2/N A3) = P(A2) - P(A3)
