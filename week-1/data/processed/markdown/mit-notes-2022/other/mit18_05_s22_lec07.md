Real-life correlations
• Over time, amount of ice cream consumption is correlated with
number of pool drownings.
• In 1685 (and today) being a student is the most dangerous
profession. That is, the average age of those who die is less than
any other profession.
• In 90% of bar fights ending in a death the person who started
the fight died.
• Hormone replacement therapy (HRT) is correlated with a lower
rate of coronary heart disease (CHD).
